What I do

A brief bit of insight into how I work. Applying creative approaches to solving complex problems.

Discovery

To start off solving any problem, one must keep an open mind to identify creative solutions. I like to draw upon experience from my diverse career background whenever approaching a new problem. This keeps the ideas fresh!

Questioning

I ask lots of questions. Sometimes I may have an answer in mind, but I love to hear others' input to build a more thorough schema of a situation. I use this information to build a model, through an iterative process.

Analysis

I try to look at the situation from every angle. Think about unexpected situations, and try to clear up ambiguity. The analysis often leads to more questions, and these parts of the process are often the most lengthy.

Report & Repeat

Portray the results of my study such that they tell a story - whether it's an app, a series of visualizations, or an authored report, the story-telling element is key for optimal communication, so that the next iteration may require less backtracking!
